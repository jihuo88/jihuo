About Jihuo

Jihuo is an adrenaline-pumping monster survival game that challenges players to test their reflexes, strategy, and endurance. Set in a dark, atmospheric world filled with dangerous creatures, players must navigate through increasingly difficult waves of enemies while collecting power-ups and upgrades to enhance their abilities.

Gameplay Mechanics

The game features intuitive controls that are easy to learn but challenging to master. Players use keyboard controls for movement and mouse controls for aiming and attacking. The game's dynamic difficulty system ensures that each playthrough presents new challenges and keeps players engaged for hours.
